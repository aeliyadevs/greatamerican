How It Works

Our hands-off approach connects individual investors with institutional grade opportunities without the exertion of underwriting deal after deal. By investing with Yankee Capital Partners you reap all the benefits of owning multifamily real estate assets without the headache of the day to day management.

YOU INVEST

Opportunity to become a part-owner in a150-to-300 unit, grade-B apartment complex, with a value of between$15 and $30 millionin the fastest growing market segment

WE OPTIMIZE

Over a five year asset hold period, we can generally achieve arental income upside of 20%while reducing our assets operating expenses by up to 25%

YOU EARN

You will receive between a5% and 7% return annuallyfrom the operations of the mulitfamily property while we strategically disburse capital to improve property and build value over a 5 to 7 hold year period. With an equity harvesting event, you will receive distribution withannualized returns ranging from 10% to 18%
